  • Education

    Stephan identified a gap where companies don’t even know this scheme exists (needs further research), or that it can be a far more efficient process He felt that businesses are often unaware of the wiggle room encompassed by “financially sound”, and that people avoid applying because they don’t want to share that they are running at a loss. Another painpoint is in the process itself - there are no examples of what an application should look like, which leads to applicant confusion. If the business isn’t applying now, they aren’t gathering the info they need in a format that they could for a future application

  • Data management

    Right now, there are 48 employees in this process, and 530 accredited companies. That’s a 1 : 10 ratio of worker per company! Already the system is strained, it’s definitely not scalable to the thousands of companies that are eligible for this process. Steffan spends 6 + hours pouring over financial documents. This intense human power is not sustainble
